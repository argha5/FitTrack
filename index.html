Privacy Policy – FitTrack

Effective Date: 2025-09-05

FitTrack ("we," "our," or "the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Information We Collect

  • No personal data collected: We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or location.
  • Local calculations and tracking only: All BMI, calories (BMR/TDEE), water intake, and goal progress calculations are done entirely on your device.
  • Local storage: Your inputs and preferences are stored locally on your device (e.g., SharedPreferences) to provide a better experience. We do not transmit this data to our servers.
  • Optional data: If you enable any future optional features like analytics or crash reporting, anonymous data such as app version, device type, or OS version may be collected to improve performance. If the app shows ads in the future, the ad network may collect non-personal information for ad personalization. We do not control this data.

2. How We Use Information

  • Improve app functionality and fix errors.
  • Provide a better user experience.
  • No personal data is sold, shared, or used for marketing.

3. Third-Party Services

  • The app may use third-party services (e.g., analytics, ads, notifications) in the future.
  • These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies.
  • We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party services used.

4. Data Security

  • Since we do not collect personal information, there is minimal risk.
  • All calculations and progress tracking are processed locally on your device.
  • Any optional analytics or crash reporting data is anonymized.

5. User Rights

  • Since we do not store personal information on our servers, there is no personal data to delete.
  • You can clear the app's local storage by uninstalling the app or using your device's app settings.
  • If you have questions about analytics or ads, you can disable them in your device settings if applicable.

6. Children’s Privacy

  • This app is not directed at children under 13.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
